Privacy Policy

Your trust matters as much as your metal. This explains what we collect, how we use it, and the simple promise behind it — we never sell or share your information.

Last updated: June 16, 2026

B.C. Bullion is a family-owned precious-metals dealer in Northwest Florida. We keep our privacy practices as straightforward as our pricing: we collect only what we need to serve you, we protect it, and we never sell it.

◆Information we collect

  • Order & contact details — your name, shipping address, phone number, and email when you place an order or reach out to us.
  • Price-alert sign-ups — the email address you give us to receive spot-price alerts and new-arrival notices.
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment and bank-wire providers. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Basic site data — standard, non-identifying analytics (such as pages viewed) that help us keep the site fast and useful.

◆How we use it

  • To process, insure, and discreetly ship your order.
  • To send the price alerts and arrival notices you asked for — nothing more.
  • To provide real, one-on-one customer service when you call or write.
  • To meet our legal, tax, and recordkeeping obligations.

◆What we never do

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share it only with the partners required to complete what you asked us to do — for example, an insured carrier to deliver your order or a payment processor to handle a transaction — and only the minimum they need.

◆How we protect it

Our site runs over encrypted (SSL) connections, and checkout is handled through PCI-compliant providers. Every order ships in plain, unmarked packaging with no indication of the contents, signature required on delivery.

◆Your choices

You can unsubscribe from price alerts at any time using the link in any email. To review, update, or delete the information we hold about you, just call us at (850) 585-1115 or email hello@bcbullion.com and a real person will take care of it.

◆Questions

If anything here is unclear, we would rather you ask than wonder. Reach us at hello@bcbullion.com or (850) 585-1115, Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm CT.

Precious metals carry market risk and can lose value; nothing here is investment, tax, or legal advice.
